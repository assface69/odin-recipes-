Casserole

Description

Basically this is the recipe of another good food; casserole, a savoury meal made with simple ingredients :).

Ingredient

  • Bacon
  • Onion
  • egg
  • Hash browns
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and onion, drain, and transfer to a bowl.
  2. Stir in the eggs, potatoes, and cheeses.
  3. Pour into a prepared baking dish and bake until the eggs are set.
